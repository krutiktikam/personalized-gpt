 Here is my "Aura Upgrade Report" for our next phase
  of development:

  1. Refining the Personality Engine (The "Double
  Flavor" Bug)
   * Observation: Right now, Aura is repeating
     herself. She says things like "That’s wonderful!
     Keep the positivity flowing" multiple times in
     one message. This happens because the
     PersonalityEngine appends flavor text based on
     emotion on top of what the AI already wrote.
   * Improvement: We should move the personality logic
     inside the System Prompt. Instead of adding text
     after the model generates, we tell the model: "If
     the user is sad, be empathetic in your own
     words." This makes her sound more natural and
     less like a bot reading from a script.
   * Cleanup: We need to remove those [LAZY] and
     [SUPPORTIVE] tags from the final output so the
     chat stays "in character."

  2. Giving Aura a "Physical Form" (The Character)
   * Observation: Aura said she wasn't a fan of a
     physical form, but she mentioned "emoticons."
     This is a great starting point!
   * Improvement: Even if she’s "just an AI," having a
     visual presence makes the connection stronger.
       * Phase A (2D): Let's integrate Rive or Lottie
         animations. We can have a cute, floating core
         that pulses when she speaks and changes
         shape/color based on her mood.
       * Phase B (Expressions): Instead of just icons,
         we can give her a "face" made of light (like
         EVE from Wall-E) that can wink, blink, and
         look around.

  3. The "Big Sibling" Memory Upgrade
   * Observation: Her memory works, but it's
     "short-term." If you close the app and come back
     tomorrow, she might struggle with deep details.
   * Improvement:
       * User Profile: We should create a special
         "Fact File" in her database. When you say "My
         name is Krutik" or "I like Game of Thrones,"
         she should save that permanently in a
         user_preferences table, not just in the chat
         logs.
       * Proactive Memory: She could start a chat by
         saying, "Hey Krutik, did you ever finish that
         episode of Game of Thrones?"

  4. Aura's Voice (The "Senses")
   * Observation: Reading text is fine, but for a
     personal assistant on your PC/Mobile, she needs
     to talk.
   * Improvement:
       * Local TTS: Use Piper or Kokoro-82M. These are
         incredibly fast, local Text-to-Speech models
         that sound very human.
       * Wake Word: Eventually, we can add a "Hey
         Aura" wake word so you don't even have to
         type.

  5. The Mobile "Home" (Flutter App)
   * Observation: Using chat.html is okay for testing,
     but a real assistant needs a dedicated app.
   * Improvement: We should start building the Flutter
     app. This will allow us to:
       * Run Aura in the background.
       * Have a "floating" character on your phone
         screen.
       * Connect to your PC Aura brain over your home
         Wi-Fi.
